Grenade

A 17th-century ceramic hand grenade from Ingolstadt, Germany. This type of grenade is used by filling it with about 130 grams of black powder and detonating it with a wooden plug. As these varied from 1.3 to 4.5 kilograms in mass, throwing them long distances is difficult.

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys for movement.

Use Q to switch between a pistol, crossbow and shotgun.

Use E to switch to a sword.

Use space or click to attack.

Use G to equip a grenade.

Use T to open the shop.

Use P to pause the game.
